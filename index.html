about me

Hey! Thanks for stopping by. I am a developer, borough adventurer, lifelong student, human jukebox, and devotee of Conan O'Brien's "work hard and be kind" mantra based in NYC.


I started my journey as a developer in the fall of 2013, when I enrolled in General Assembly's Web Development Immersive program.


Before that, I was an investment banking analyst at UBS and Greentech Capital Advisors, focusing on the renewable energy and infrastructure sectors.


I graduated from the University of Pennsylvania's Wharton School in 2011, with concentrations in Finance and Operations and Information Management. At Penn, I was in the Mask and Wig Club where I performed in the cast and played drums, guitar, and keys in the band.
